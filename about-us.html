Welcome to Altan Mendi

Altan Mendi is a modern food-ordering website designed to bring the rich taste of Mendi — a traditional fragrant rice dish served with tender, perfectly spiced meat — straight to your table.

Whether you crave juicy Chicken Mendi or succulent Beef Mendi, we make it simple to browse, customize your spices and sides, and order your favorite plate online with fast delivery.

Rooted in authentic Ethiopian and Arabian culinary traditions, every plate is prepared fresh with love, using quality ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.
